📥 Get your full LinkedIn export (the richest single source)

  1. On LinkedIn: Me → Settings & Privacy.
  2. Data privacy → Get a copy of your data.
  3. Pick “Want something in particular? Select the data files you’re most interested in” and tick Profile, Positions, Education, Skills, Certifications, Honors, Recommendations, Languages (or just request the larger full archive).
  4. Click Request archive. LinkedIn emails a download link — often within ~10 minutes; the complete archive can take up to 24 hours.
  5. Download the .zip and upload it in step 1 above — it’s filed into your dossier’s linkedin/ folder.
